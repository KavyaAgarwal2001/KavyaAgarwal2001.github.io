My Life List

I recently watched the movie The Life List, so you could say that inspired me to start writing mine. I’ll try to update it every few weeks — though we’ll see if that actually happens.

This is a list of things I want to do, learn, feel, or create in this one short life on this big ball of rock (and yeah, it’s safe to assume I’ll never physically go to outer space — I neither have the means nor the will for that).
